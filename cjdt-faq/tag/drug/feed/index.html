Would FDLE consider amending/expanding the drug type table to include more categories/types? For instance, the SA data dictionary includes heroin, morphine, and opium, but doesn’t list other opioids that are not opium based, such as synthetic opioids.

Answer

FDLE has standardized drug types based on the FBI National Incident-Based Reporting System (NIBRS) Drug Indicators.
